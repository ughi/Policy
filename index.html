This page explains how users of the Gluwa app can request the deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account, please send an email to:

marketing@gluwa.com

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your User ID
  • The email address associated with your account (if applicable)
  • Any additional information that may help us identify your account

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (such as User ID and profile data)
  • Application usage data associated with your account

Data Retention

Certain data may be retained for legal, security, or operational purposes:

  • Server logs (retained for up to 30 days)

After the retention period, all remaining data will be permanently deleted.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days after we receive your request.

Important Notes

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding account deletion, please contact us at marketing@gluwa.com.
